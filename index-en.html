About Me

Cross-disciplinary Explorer of Psychology and Interaction Design

I have an interdisciplinary background in psychology and human-computer interaction design, which enables me to create truly meaningful product experiences from the perspective of users' psychological needs. My design process integrates user research, behavioral analysis, and interaction design principles to ensure products are both beautiful and practical.

Over the past 5 years, I have participated in multiple design projects across different fields, from mobile applications to web design, and serious game development. I am passionate about exploring how design can solve real problems and focus on the impact of design on user behavior and psychological states.
